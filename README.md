## Glimt Breathwork Coach (SKILL.md)

This is the repo for the [Glimt macOS app](https://www.glimtapp.io?utm_source=github_skill) agent skill integration.

Setup your agent to remind you to breathe — when your stressed, its running a long task or when you ask it to!

### Glimt

You probably breathe fine when you're relaxed. It's the other hours — deep in a build, grinding through a review, stuck in back-to-back meetings — where it gets shallow, fast, and forgotten.

Glimt lives in your menu bar. It watches your day quietly. At whatever interval feels right to you, it floats a gentle breathing prompt over your screen, guides you through a round of Box Breathing, 4-7-8, or Just Breathe, and disappears when you're done. You go back to work. Calmer.

That's it. No apps to open. No routines to remember.

It also connects to the macOS Shortcuts app, so your environment can shift with you. Start a session and your music pauses, your Focus mode flips, Slack goes to BRB — whatever you've set up. When you're done breathing, everything comes back. No friction, no manual switching.

Smart Pause holds reminders automatically whenever any app is using your microphone or camera. You'll never get interrupted mid-call.

No subscription. No data collected. No attention hijacked. Just breath.
